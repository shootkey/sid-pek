the pek people
Pek came about when a bunch of animators and artists decided to pool their experiences, back in the summer of 2020. Our work has ranged across entertainment, branded content and commercials; all with a shared goal of communicating with elegance.

We're driven to push the boundaries of visual storytelling with technology and imagination, while keeping true to our traditional roots - creating entire worlds on a blank piece of paper to begin with.

More than an animation studio, Pek is a shared playground for us; when it gets muddy, we rejoice.
